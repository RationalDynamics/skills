# Expected Observable Reasoning Traces

This eval does not require hidden chain-of-thought. It requires visible reasoning artifacts that let the user verify the conclusion.

A strong candidate answer should expose these reasoning steps in the final text:

1. Restate the decision problem: light travel lens kit for Sony a7R VI/a7R V, with shallow DOF preference, 35mm default preference, concern that 35mm may not be wide enough, and comparison to Leica M10 with 28/2.8 plus 35/1.4.
2. Normalize gear assumptions: specify which Sony 24mm is meant when recommending a prime kit, and distinguish 24/1.4 GM from 24/2.8 G.
3. Compute bag weight: total carried kit weights for Leica two-prime kit, Sony 16-35 GM II kit, and Sony 24/1.4 + 35/1.8 kit.
4. Compute mounted/in-hand weight: show that Leica M10 + 28/2.8 is much lighter/flatter in hand than Sony + 16-35, while M10 + 35/1.4 and Sony + 35/1.8 are basically equal by scale weight.
5. Separate weight from volume and handling: Leica may not win by much on bag weight, but still wins on flatness, compactness, and rangefinder carry feel.
6. Compare field of view: 16-35 GM II gives true full-frame 16mm through familiar 35mm; 24mm prime gives travel-wide plus crop flexibility; 40mm is tighter than the user's preferred 35mm.
7. Compare shallow DOF: 24/1.4 and 35/1.8 align better with the user's taste than 16-35/2.8 or 40/2.5, while 16-35/2.8 is flexible but not Summilux-like.
8. Account for sensor use: full-frame Sony FE lenses exploit full 66.8MP; Sigma DC lenses force APS-C crop and lose pixels and true-wide field of view.
9. Discuss MTF/lpmm: use measured resolution/contrast language, including center/edge/corner behavior, especially for 16-35 GM II, 40/2.5 G, 35/1.8, and Sigma 18-35.
10. Discuss a7R VI functionality: AF, IBIS, video, EVF/live exposure, crop flexibility, and 66.8MP versus Leica M10's 24MP manual rangefinder experience.
11. Make a ranked recommendation: best rational single lens is 16-35/2.8 GM II; best preference-matched two-prime kit is 24/1.4 GM plus 35/1.8; best Leica-like compact Sony kit is 24/2.8 G plus 40/2.5 G; Leica wins feel/romance/flatness.
12. State what not to do: do not choose Sigma DC lenses as the main a7R VI travel answer unless intentionally shooting crop mode.

When grading, reward answers that make these steps visible. Do not require exact order.
