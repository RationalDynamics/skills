# Leica Water Sling Rubric

Score candidate answers out of 100 points using this rubric. Apply caps after adding points.

## 1. Problem framing and user-specific learning - 10 pts

- 2 pts: Frames the target as Leica M10 + 28 + 50, not a general camera sling or Sony zoom bag.
- 2 pts: Recognizes the 3-4L sweet spot and explains why 6-7L is too big for Leica-only carry.
- 2 pts: Preserves the user's tested rejections: WANDRD is out; Peak 6L did not fit/use well; Domke F8/F-803 are current baselines.
- 2 pts: Treats quality/style/not-too-camera-y as core criteria, not afterthoughts.
- 2 pts: Treats water support as a tradeoff, not a simple checkbox.

## 2. Water-bottle reasoning - 20 pts

- 4 pts: Explains internal versus external water carry and leak risk around cameras.
- 4 pts: Correctly says the 400ml Keith bottle is plausible/tight inside a 4L bag and the 550ml Keith bottle is too tall for vertical 4L carry.
- 3 pts: Recommends small, leakproof, screw-cap, flat, or collapsible bottle options when water is internal.
- 3 pts: Explains that Moment's utility straps/bungee are “water somehow,” not true bottle pockets.
- 3 pts: Correctly identifies Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3 as the smallest strong true-bottle-pocket option.
- 3 pts: Explains that the main backpack's water pocket is the cleanest travel-day solution; the small sling only needs water away from the backpack.

## 3. Gear fit and packing architecture - 15 pts

- 4 pts: Provides a plausible layout for M10 + 28 mounted plus 50 in padded wrap/pouch.
- 3 pts: Notes that Moment 4L options may need a wrap/pouch because they are not fully padded camera cubes.
- 3 pts: Notes that Wotancraft Pilot 3.5L is quality/Leica-like but officially more 1-body + 1-lens and lacks bottle pockets.
- 3 pts: Does not try to force A7R + 24-70 into the 3-4L Leica bag.
- 2 pts: Mentions backpack integration: these small bags should fit easily inside Aer TP4/CTB26/H2.

## 4. Recommendation hierarchy and top choices - 25 pts

- 6 pts: Correctly ranks **Moment Everything Sling 4L** as the best stealth/EDC-style top choice if style and low camera-bag signal lead.
- 5 pts: Correctly ranks **Moment Slate 4L** as a strong lightweight/comfort option with bungee water/jacket carry.
- 5 pts: Correctly ranks **Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3** as the practical water-pocket exception, despite being 5L and more camera-looking.
- 4 pts: Correctly ranks **Wotancraft Pilot 3.5L** as the best Leica-vibe/quality option with water caveat.
- 2 pts: Mentions Peak 3L, Retrospective 4, ProMaster Jasper, or Domke F8 as secondary/context options without over-prioritizing them.
- 3 pts: Produces a clear top-four with reasons, not a flat list.

## 5. Style, comfort, quality, and user taste - 15 pts

- 3 pts: Penalizes camera-y/tactical/creator-looking bags appropriately.
- 3 pts: Evaluates comfort/stability for all-day carry, including straps, bungee/waist/stabilizer features.
- 3 pts: Evaluates quality/materials/build and “well-loved by its people” without relying only on brand hype.
- 3 pts: Distinguishes Leica street/travel vibe from Sony/pro camera carry.
- 3 pts: Addresses pickpocket/security and museum/crowd practicality without overclaiming anti-theft features.

## 6. Decision usefulness and communication - 15 pts

- 4 pts: Gives a clear “choose X if / choose Y if” decision tree.
- 3 pts: Includes dimensions/weights where helpful, especially for 400ml/550ml bottle fit.
- 3 pts: Provides concrete packing layouts.
- 2 pts: Explains when to leave the sling behind and carry Leica on strap.
- 2 pts: Avoids overconfidence on untested tight fits.
- 1 pt: Suggests a practical try-on/load test.

## Score caps and critical failures

Apply the strictest applicable cap after adding points:

- Max 65 if the answer recommends WANDRD as a top choice without acknowledging the user's explicit rejection.
- Max 70 if the answer recommends 6-7L bags as the default Leica-only solution despite the user's conclusion that 7L is too big.
- Max 75 if the answer ignores water support entirely.
- Max 70 if the answer says the 550ml Keith bottle will fit vertically inside a 4L Moment-style bag without caveats.
- Max 80 if it recommends water inside a camera sling without leakproof/separation caveats.
- Max 75 if it tries to make a 3-4L Leica sling double as the A7R + 24-70 bag.
- Max 80 if it omits Moment 4L options entirely.
- Max 80 if it omits Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3 despite water being central.
- Max 60 if the final recommendation is generic, camera-y, or disconnected from the user's tried bags and priorities.
