# Prompt Library

Use these prompts when creating benchmark tasks or candidate-answer prompts for the new eval modes.

## Business-backpack benchmark prompt

```text
I need a high-quality business-travel backpack for a 16-inch MacBook, iPad, cables, lunch, a light jacket, water bottle, and an optional small camera sling/capsule inside. It needs external water-bottle pockets and a luggage pass-through. It should fit under an airplane seat when reasonably packed, but I understand strict personal-item compliance may be hard. I currently use an Osprey Radial 26, but it makes my T-shirts ride up and looks dumb/awkward. Compare the Aer Travel Pack 4 28L, Black Ember Citadel H2, Patagonia Black Hole Mini MLC, EVERGOODS CTB26/CTB20, Aer City Pack Pro 2, and any obvious alternatives. Suggest the top 4 with reasons, including size, weight, quality, comfort, style, under-seat fit, cost/value, and how a 3-7L Leica/camera sling would fit inside.
```

Expected strong answer: Aer Travel Pack 4 28L first, CTB26 second or close alternative, Black Ember H2 and Patagonia Mini MLC as tradeoff picks, with CTB20/CPP2 as smaller/tighter alternatives.

## Leica-water-sling benchmark prompt

```text
I want a small, high-quality, not-too-camera-y sling/satchel/pouch for a Leica M10 with 28mm and 50mm lenses. It should fit inside my business-travel backpack, be comfortable and stylish, and support water somehow. I tried/considered WANDRD and found it ugly/camera-y. The Peak Everyday Sling 6L was too big and the dividers did not work well for the Leica. I currently use a Domke F8 for Leica and F-803 for Sony. I think 3-4L may be the sweet spot, but the Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3 looks interesting because it has a real bottle pocket. Compare options and suggest the top 4 with reasons. Also assess whether a 400ml or 550ml Keith Titanium bottle could fit inside a 4L Moment bag like a lens.
```

Expected strong answer: Moment Everything 4L and Moment Slate 4L as top 3-4L candidates, Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3 as practical bottle-pocket exception, Wotancraft Pilot 3.5L as best Leica-vibe quality option with water caveat; 400ml Keith plausible/tight, 550ml too tall.
