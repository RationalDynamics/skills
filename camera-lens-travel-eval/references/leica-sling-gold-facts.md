# Leica Water Sling Gold Facts And Expected Conclusions

This file defines the factual baseline for the Leica/water sling eval. The evaluator should grade candidate answers against these facts, allowing small rounding differences and noting that prices can change.

## User's normalized decision problem

The user wants a small, high-quality, stylish, comfortable, not-too-camera-y bag for:

- Leica M10.
- Leica 28mm lens, usually mounted.
- Leica 50mm lens as a spare.
- Water support somehow.
- Comfortable all-day carry.
- Fit inside a larger business/travel backpack such as Aer Travel Pack 4 28L, CTB26, Black Ember H2, or similar.

The user has learned through testing that 6-7L camera bags are usually too big for Leica-only carry unless the sling also needs water, jacket, or Sony crossover capability. The emerging sweet spot is **3-4L**, with the Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3 as the smallest compelling exception because it has a real bottle pocket.

## Important user-specific history

A strong answer must preserve these discoveries:

- The user currently uses a **Domke F8** for Leica and an **F-803** for Sony.
- The Domke F8 likely fits inside business backpacks but lacks water support and is not especially comfortable.
- The user tried/considered **WANDRD** and rejected it as ugly and too camera-bag-y.
- The user tried/considered **Peak Everyday Sling 6L** and found it too large for Leica, with dividers that do not handle the Leica well; it also was too short for A7R + 24-70 lens-down.
- The user suspects 3-4L is the sweet spot for Leica-only carry.
- 7L bags such as Wotancraft Pilot 7L or compagnon Element 7L are good when adding water/jacket/Sony crossover, but are more bag than needed for Leica M10 + 28 + 50.
- A bag should not look like a tactical/creator/camera bag if avoidable.

## Expected recommendation logic

A strong answer should converge on one of these two pathways:

### If the answer emphasizes small, stylish, not-camera-y Leica carry

1. **Moment Everything Sling 4L** — best stealth/EDC top pick. Use built-in divider plus a padded wrap/pouch for the 50mm; use bottom utility straps for small bottle/jacket or a 400ml/flat bottle inside only with leak precautions.
2. **Moment Slate 4L** — best lightweight/comfort pick with bungee bottle/jacket carry and stability strap.
3. **Wotancraft Pilot 3.5L** — best Leica vibe/quality, but water remains unsolved without an add-on or the main backpack.
4. **Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3** — best if real water-bottle pocket and quick rotation matter more than staying 3-4L and not looking camera-y.

### If the answer emphasizes real water-bottle support

1. **Think Tank TurnStyle 5L V3** — smallest practical true bottle-pocket camera sling.
2. **Moment Everything Sling 4L** — good if using utility straps or a small/flat/400ml internal bottle.
3. **Moment Slate 4L** — good lightweight bungee-carry alternative.
4. **Wotancraft Pilot 3.5L** — only if water is handled elsewhere.

## Required caveats

A high-scoring answer should state:

- Do not try to make the 3-4L Leica sling also fit A7R + 24-70; keep Sony in the F-803 or a larger bag.
- If carrying water inside a small camera bag, use a leakproof screw-cap or flat/collapsible bottle and isolate it from camera gear.
- The 400ml Keith bottle is plausible; the 550ml is too tall for vertical carry in 4L.
- External water on the main backpack is the cleanest travel-day solution; the small sling only needs water when used away from the backpack.
- True museum mode may still be Leica on strap + spare lens in pocket/pouch, not a sling full of water and gear.
